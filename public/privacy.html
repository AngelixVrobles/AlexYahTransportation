Privacy Policy

At AlexyYah Transportation, we take the privacy of our applicants seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide when applying to become a driver with our company. By using our website and submitting your information, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you apply to become a driver, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name and contact details (phone number, email address, and home address)
  • Driver’s license information and driving history
  • Vehicle details, including proof of insurance
  • Background check and employment history information
  • Any additional documents or details required for compliance with safety standards

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To evaluate your application and determine your eligibility as a driver
  • To contact you regarding your application or related opportunities
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements related to student transportation
  • To ensure the safety and reliability of our services

3. Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information will only be shared under the following circumstances:

  • With authorized staff members directly involved in the recruitment and onboarding process
  • If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process (e.g., court order)
  • To protect the safety, rights, and property of our company, our drivers, and the students we serve

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we strive to protect your data to the best of our ability.

5. Data Retention

We retain applicant information only as long as necessary to complete the recruitment and compliance process, or as required by law. Once the information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Rights

As an applicant, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, where legally applicable
  • Withdraw your application at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
