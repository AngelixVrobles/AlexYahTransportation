About Us

We provide alternative transportation to and from school for Special Education children. Our mission is to ensure that every student travels safely, comfortably, and on time.

We are currently looking for responsible and caring drivers to help transport students to and from school.

How it work

We provide alternative transportation for special education students to and from school.

Why Us?

We provide competitive salary with flexible schedule.

Drive with US

Select a state to view available cities.

Benefits

  • Flexible part-time schedule with competitive compensation
  • $30 base fare for distances between 1 and 10 miles
  • $1.25 additional per mile after the first 10 miles
  • Reliable and consistent work schedule
  • Commercial Driver's License (CDL) not required

Driver Requirements

  • 01 Pass a National Background Check
  • 02 Have a Clean Driving Record
  • 03 Own a 4-Door, Insured Vehicle
  • 04 Carry a Valid Driver’s License
  • 05 Complete Student Safety Training
  • 06 Pass a Live Interview & Orientation
  • 07 Maintain Excellent Ratings from Families
